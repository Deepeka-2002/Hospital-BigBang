Overview

The DocPlus group of hospitals hopes to see more people in India and around the world getting non-compromised, evidence-based healthcare. Working towards this end, Be Well has been setting up hospitals with world-class infrastructure in locations that currently have limited access to healthcare. Doc Plus is a chain of "Small Giant" Multispeciality hospitals creating the most innovative healthcare infrastructure for small towns in India. It spreads health awareness in engaging ways that shift the community focus from "Get Well" to "Be Well"
